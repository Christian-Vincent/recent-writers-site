My Favorite Recent Writers

Created by artist for Reedsy

Madeline Miller

Having only published two books, her works mainly focus on the retelling of Greek myths.

Her work includes:

  • The Song of Achilles
  • Circe

Margaret Atwood

Her works not only include novels, but short stories and poems as well. She defined a new type of dystopia while speaking through a strong female viewpoint.

Her work includes:

  • The Handmaid's Tale
  • The Testament
  • Surfacing
  • Alias Grace

Patrick Rothfuss

A legendary author rivaling George R. R. Martin (for all the wrong reasons), he has enjoyed one of the most successful debuts of any writer out there.

His work includes:

  • The Name of the Wind
  • The Wise Man's Fear
  • The Slow Regard of Silent Things

These writers have shown that patience and persistence are what makes great stories.

Writer Story
Madeline Miller Wrote a first draft of "The Song of Achilles" over the course of five years before scrapping the entire thing and starting over
Margaret Atwood Despite being self-described as a terrible speller, she has continued to work through that weakness and write what she enjoys
Patrick Rothfuss Spent ten years writing The Kingkiller Chronicle before getting an agent

If there is one thing to take away from this article, it is that anything worth doing takes time, persistence, and a desire to keep moving forward!
